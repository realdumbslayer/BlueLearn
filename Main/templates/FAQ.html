How can we help you?

We're here to assist you! If you've already found the answer you're looking for, fantastic! If not, feel free to reach out to us via email for personalized assistance.

To sign up for an account please follow these steps:

  • Go to our website's homepage.
  • Look for the "Join Now" button, typically located at the top right corner of the page.
  • Click on the "Register"link.
  • Fill out the registration form with the required information, such as your name, email address, role, and password.
  • Agree to the terms of service and any other policies, if prompted.
  • Once you've successfully signed up, you should be redirected to the login page.

  • Go to the login page of our website.
  • Click on the "Forgot Password?" link.
  • Enter your email address associated with your account.
  • Enter your security question and answer associated with your account.
  • Once submitted successfully, follow the instructions to create a new password..
  • Once you've successfully reset your password, you can log in with your new credentials.

If you've been provided with a course enrollment code by your instructor, follow these steps:

  • Log in to your account on our website.
  • Find the option to "Enroll in a Course" or "Join a Class."
  • Enter the enrollment code provided by your instructor.
  • Click on the "Enroll" or "Join" button.
  • You should now be enrolled in the course and have access to its content.
